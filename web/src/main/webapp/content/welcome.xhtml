Welcome to the Customary Tenure Certificates of Ownership (CCO) Database

Using this CCO database, you can secure legal documentation, through registration of your customary rights in Uganda. A certificate of customary ownership is conclusive evidence of ownership as an individual, family and community.

Applicants can register and view applications . User registration can be done online and once you complete registration Community ICT officer will review it and approve. Upon approval a confirmation code will be sent to your e-mail address. You will then need to activate your registration request, by following the received link. Another way is to contact your Community ICT officer and they will enroll you as a registered user.

All Applicants registrations are subject to review and approval by the Community ICT officer and the Community Moderation Committee.

If you are already registered, use Login page to login into the system.
